How Countries Feel about a More Connected Future

Welcome to a Visualization on Mozilla Survey Data!

The coloring of each of the countries depicts the way most of the people in that country feel about a more connected Future. The legend is shown below. Clicking on a country will display the number of total surveys submitted by that country. The exact survey prompt posed to the user is as followed. "Thinking about a future in which so much of your world is connected to the internet leaves you feeling"

  • Orange: Scared as hell
  • Blue: A little wary
  • Green: On the fence
  • Purple: Cautiously optimistic
  • Red: Super excited!
  • Pink: No Survey Data
